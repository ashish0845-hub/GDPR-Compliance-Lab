# Cookie Policy

This Resource Booking System uses cookies to ensure the application functions correctly and securely.

## 1. What are Cookies?
Cookies are small text files stored on your browser. This application uses them primarily for session management.

## 2. Essential Cookies (Strictly Necessary)
These cookies are required for the system to function. They cannot be turned off.
* **Session ID:** Used to keep you logged in as you navigate between the booking page and your profile (Spec 2).
* **Role Verification:** Helps the system remember if you are a "Reserver" or an "Administrator" (Spec 3).
* **CSRF Token:** A security cookie used to prevent unauthorized actions being taken on your behalf.

## 3. Non-Essential Cookies
* **Analytics/Tracking:** We do **not** use any third-party tracking or advertising cookies.
* **Marketing:** We do **not** use cookies for marketing purposes.

## 4. Duration
The cookies used are **Session Cookies**. This means they are temporary and are typically deleted from your device once you close your web browser (Spec 1).

## 5. Managing Cookies
You can block cookies in your browser settings, but please note that you will not be able to log in or reserve resources if you do so.
