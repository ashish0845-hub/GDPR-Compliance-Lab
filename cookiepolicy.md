# Cookie Policy

## 1. What are cookies?
Cookies are small text files stored in your browser to help the website function correctly.

## 2. How we use cookies
Our system uses **Strictly Necessary Cookies** for the following purposes:
* **Authentication (Spec 2):** To identify you as a logged-in user and distinguish between a Reserver and an Administrator.
* **Security:** To protect your session from unauthorized access.

## 3. Types of Cookies Used
| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration | Type |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| `session_id` | Maintains your login state across pages. | Session | First-party |
| `csrf_token` | Prevents Cross-Site Request Forgery attacks. | Session | Security |

## 4. Your Choices
Because these cookies are strictly necessary for the system to function (you cannot log in without them), they do not require prior consent under GDPR. However, you can block them via your browser settings, which will disable the booking features of the site.

## 5. Third-Party Cookies
We do not use any third-party tracking, advertising, or analytics cookies.
