# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** March 2026

## 1. Introduction
Our Resource Reservation System is committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains how we handle your information in accordance with the GDPR.

## 2. Data We Collect
* **Account Data:** Name and email address for registration and login (Spec 2).
* **Age Verification:** Confirmation that you are over 15 years old (Spec 6).
* **Usage Data:** Details of your resource reservations (Spec 7).

## 3. Legal Basis for Processing
We process your data based on **Contractual Necessity** (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR)—we need this information to provide the booking service you requested.

## 4. Data Sharing and Visibility
* **Administrators:** Have access to modify or delete your data to manage the system (Spec 4 & 5).
* **Public View:** Booked resources are visible publicly, but your identity is strictly hidden (Spec 8).

## 5. Your Rights
Under the GDPR, you have the right to:
* **Access** your data.
* **Rectify** incorrect data.
* **Erasure:** You may request that the Administrator delete your account (Spec 5).

## 6. Security
Following **Privacy by Design** (Spec 10), we implement technical measures to ensure your data is stored securely and accessed only by authorized roles.
